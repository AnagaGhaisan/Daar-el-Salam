About Us

Islamic Inclusion School

Since 2004 Daar El-Salam Middle School has also participated in efforts to educate the nation's children. Our commitment is to create a generation of Muslims who are superior, have high academic abilities, are personable, have empathy for their environment, are well established in science and uphold the Al-Quran and As-Sunnah. To achieve this commitment, we develop a school system that is superior, visionary, produces students who are quality, devout, and uphold the Al-Qur'an and As-Sunnah. As well as trying to comprehensively understand every educational problem while looking for alternative solutions by developing sustainable improvements.

One of the reasons for this commitment is the understanding that every student has the right to receive quality education. Education that is able to accommodate the diversity of students' abilities in achieving their learning achievements. Therefore, inclusive education is the right solution to overcome today's learning problems. Through inclusive education, classrooms change from low-tech to high-tech. Thus encouraging a combination of traditional learning models (face to face) which emphasize interaction and participation in the classroom as well as learning models that integrate innovation and technological advances through online learning systems (hybrid learning).

Vision & Mission

Vision

The realization of superior schools that give birth to superior Islamic generations, who have high academic abilities, are personable, have empathy for their environment, are well established in science and adhere to the Al-Quran and As-Sunnah.

Mission 1

Developing a school system that is superior, visionary, produces students who are quality, devout, and uphold the Al-Qur'an and As-Sunnah.

Mission 2

Striving to comprehensively understand every educational problem while looking for alternative solutions by developing sustainable improvements.

Mission 3

Carry out learning and guidance effectively, so that each student develops optimally according to their potential.

Mission 4

Intensively foster a spirit of excellence among all school members.

Mission 5

Encourage and help each student to recognize his or her potential, so that it can be developed optimally.

Mission 6

Developing appreciation and practice of Islamic values so that they become a source of wisdom and thought and action.

Mission 7

Implement participatory management by involving all school residents and related parties.
